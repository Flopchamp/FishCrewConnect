## 💳 Payment Process Summary

### For Boat Owners (Paying)
1. ✅ Complete job successfully
2. 💰 Navigate to payment section
3. 📝 Enter payment amount and fisherman's phone
4. 📱 Confirm via M-Pesa STK Push
5. ✅ Payment automatically sent to fisherman

### For Fishermen (Receiving)
1. ✅ Complete job successfully
2. ⏳ Wait for boat owner to initiate payment
3. 📱 Receive M-Pesa notification
4. 💰 Money appears in M-Pesa account (minus 5% platform fee)
5. ✅ Confirmation in app

### Payment Fees
- **Platform Commission**: 5% of total payment
- **M-Pesa Fees**: Standard M-Pesa transaction fees apply
- **Payment Method**: Only M-Pesa supported

## 🆘 Emergency Quick Fixes

### App Not Working
1. 🔄 Force close and restart app
2. 📶 Check internet connection
3. 🔄 Restart your device
4. 📲 Update app in app store

### Can't Log In
1. ✅ Verify email and password
2. 🔓 Use "Forgot Password" option
3. 📧 Check email for reset link
4. 📞 Contact support if locked out

### Payment Issues
1. ⏰ Wait 30 minutes for processing
2. 📱 Check M-Pesa account status
3. ✅ Verify phone number in profile
4. 📞 Contact support for assistance

### Messaging Problems
1. 📶 Check internet connection
2. ✅ Ensure you've applied to/accepted the job
3. 🔄 Restart app
4. 📞 Contact support if persists
